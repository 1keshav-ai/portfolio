About Me

Hello! I'm Keshav Kumar Jha, a passionate and detail-oriented software developer and web designer based in Greater Noida. Currently pursuing my MCA at G.L BAJAJ Institute of Technology and Management, I have a keen interest in building efficient, scalable, and user-friendly digital solutions.


I have experience working with a variety of technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, and I’ve also explored tools like Bootstrap, GitHub, Postman, and Figma. Whether it’s creating engaging web designs or ensuring software runs smoothly through testing, I enjoy every step of the development lifecycle.


I approach every project with dedication and a mindset for continuous learning. My goal is not just to build websites, but to deliver real value through thoughtful design, clean code, and reliable functionality.


Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring new tech trends, watching tutorials, and improving my skills through hands-on projects and collaborations.
